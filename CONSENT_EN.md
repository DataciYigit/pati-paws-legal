**3. Purposes of Data Processing**

• AI-powered health analysis 

• GPS-based walk tracking

• Personalized food recommendations 

• Lost-dog reporting and mapping 

• Partner vet/shop suggestions 

• Notifications and alerts **4. AI Processing** 

Pati Paws uses AI \(Groq API\) to generate advanced pet wellness analyses. 

AI receives:
